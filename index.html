The analysis ladder

Take your data exactly as far as the question needs — pay for the stages you want.

1 · Primary

raw data → analysis-ready

Demultiplexing, adapter/quality trimming, alignment to genome, and read-level + alignment QC — soft-clips, chimeric reads, split-strand (hairpin/foldback) artefacts, duplicates, coverage.

2 · Secondary

alignments → features

Variant calling & filtering (germline or somatic), structural & chromosomal rearrangements, transcript quantification and sashimi plots — benchmarked where a truth set exists.

3 · Tertiary

features → biology

Differential expression (up/down-regulation), population genetics, and statistical modelling — effects, correlations, and variance-partition driver analysis.
